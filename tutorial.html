How to Setup Your Bot

01

Create an Account

First, you need a trading account on a supported broker. Make sure your account is fully verified before depositing funds.

02

Connect THINz බණ්ඩා

Link your broker's API keys to the THINz බණ්ඩා interface. We use secure encryption so your funds are never directly accessible to us.

03

Set Risk Limits (Crucial)

Before launching the bot, always configure your Daily Stop Loss and Take Profit limits. For beginners, we recommend risking no more than 1-2% of your capital per day.

04

Start Trading!

Activate the bot and monitor its performance. Do not interfere with trades manually while the bot is running to avoid conflicting strategies.
